About Us

A sweet attempt to treat the gourmets royally is the soul ambition of Lovely's Cake Fouzi, Cake maker by passion. Each bit of her cake is an escape from that monotonous sweetness and harmful preservatives. They are 100% healthy and exceptionally delicious.

Mixed with ethnic virtues and novel thoughts, Fouzi bakes premium party cakes to make the moments memorable and celebrations celestial. Her passion for cakes made her travel many countries in search of new flavours and technologies in cake industry. She has attended many national and international cake festivals with a vision to make her cakes impeccable. And she is the first person introducing different and delicious flavours popular in foreign countries.
